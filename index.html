WeatherPro Privacy Policy & NDA

Last Updated: Feb 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Location data (for forecasts)

  • Usage analytics (if enabled)
  • Preferences (units, saved locations)

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide weather forecasts and alerts
  • Improve app performance
  • Support and troubleshooting

3. Sharing

Location data processed in real time. Preferences retained until you delete the app.

We use HTTPS and standard security practices.

If you are a beta tester, you agree that pre-release features, performance data, UI changes, bugs, and roadmap details are confidential. You won't share screenshots or recordings without written permission. This NDA lasts 12 months after the feature launches or beta ends.
